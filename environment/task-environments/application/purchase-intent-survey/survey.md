# Purchase-intent survey

Read `product.md` first. Answer all six questions as yourself, weighing the
actual details and the change described there. For the five multiple-choice
questions, copy the exact answer code into your JSON — do not paraphrase.

## q1 — purchase_intent

How likely are you to buy the product given the change?

- `definitely_would_buy`
- `probably_would_buy`
- `might_or_might_not`
- `probably_would_not`
- `definitely_would_not`

## q2 — price_fairness

How do you perceive the price relative to the product's value now?

- `much_too_high`
- `somewhat_high`
- `about_right`
- `good_value`
- `great_value`

## q3 — alternative_seeking

Would you look for a competing product or brand instead, because of this change?

- `yes`
- `no`

## q4 — purchase_timing

When, if ever, would you make this purchase given the change?

- `buy_now`
- `wait_for_sale`
- `not_planning_to_buy`

## q5 — necessity_level

How essential is this purchase to you right now?

- `essential`
- `important_but_not_urgent`
- `nice_to_have`

## q6 — reasoning

One to three sentences, in your own words, explaining your decision — grounded
in your own situation and this specific product. Not generic filler.
