# Notification Management

How people receive, control, and feel about notifications across their phones, computers, and wearables.

## About this survey

This survey measures notification volume, which app types are allowed to notify, and how proactively people manage permissions and quiet times. It covers behaviors such as Do Not Disturb and Focus modes, scheduled silencing, response speed, dismissing without reading, and handling of desktop and browser notifications. It uses agreement-scale Likert batteries on overload and focus, a per-item disruptiveness matrix across messaging, social, email, news, promotional, and work notifications, a "top 3 goals" ranking, and numeric free-text items on apps silenced and notification-driven checks. It closes with satisfaction, future intent, a 0-10 control scale, and a full demographics block including age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, household, region, locale, phone platform, and tech skill.
