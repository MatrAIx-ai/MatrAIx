# CI/CD Adoption

How software teams adopt and operate continuous integration and continuous delivery pipelines.

## About this survey

This survey measures the adoption and practice of CI/CD on software teams, opening with a screener on maturity, tenure, and the respondent's relationship to pipelines. It records the primary and secondary tools in use and the specifics of what pipelines automate, deployment frequency and targets, trigger types, delivery automation level, deployment and rollback strategies, test coverage, and infrastructure-as-code usage. Attitudes are captured through an agreement Likert battery about speed, reliability, and quality and a per-item importance matrix over tool-selection criteria, plus a top-three ranking of CI/CD benefits and a multi-select of challenges. Additional sections address failure and incident frequency, monitoring and security integration, runner model, and satisfaction with speed and reliability, followed by overall satisfaction, recommendation likelihood, investment intent, and switching intent. Question types include single-choice, multi-select, Likert scales, an importance matrix, a ranking item, and numeric free-text. The survey concludes with a demographic block covering role, professional experience, employment, company size, industry, age, gender, race and ethnicity, education, U.S. region, and urbanicity.
