# Antivirus Software

How people choose, pay for, and use antivirus and security software to protect their personal devices.

## About this survey

This survey covers antivirus and endpoint-security behavior: whether respondents rely on built-in operating-system protection or a third-party product, which brands they use (Microsoft Defender, Norton, McAfee, Bitdefender, Malwarebytes, Kaspersky, Avast, and others), which devices they protect, and how they acquired the software. It measures annual spending, scan frequency, features used, and past malware infections, then uses Likert batteries on trust, value, and performance impact plus an importance matrix on selection criteria. Further sections probe threat worry, alert behavior, research channels, reasons for going without, a top-3 values ranking, and outcome measures for satisfaction, likelihood to recommend, and renewal intent. A comprehensive demographic block captures age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, urbanicity, tech comfort, and relationship to tech work.
