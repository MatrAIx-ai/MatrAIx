# Video Streaming Services

How people subscribe to and watch video streaming services for movies and TV, including which services they use, spending, and what drives their choices and cancellations.

## About this survey

This survey covers video streaming behavior: which services respondents subscribe to, their most-watched service, devices used, hours watched, monthly spend, and plan type (ad-free versus ad-supported). It measures what and how people watch, discovery methods, binge-watching and account sharing, and attitudes toward value, service overload, price sensitivity, and ads through Likert scales. An importance battery covers the drivers of service choice, and a top-3 ranking captures cancellation triggers, alongside subscription rotation, offline downloads, recommendation satisfaction, and future spending intent. The question types include single-choice, multi-select, Likert and importance batteries, a ranking question, a 0–10 recommendation scale, and numeric free-text. Demographics captured include age, gender, race or ethnicity, household income, education, employment status, household size, presence of children, U.S. region, and urban/suburban/rural setting.
