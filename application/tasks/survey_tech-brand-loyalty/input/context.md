# Tech Brand Loyalty

How loyal consumers are to their technology brands and ecosystems, and what keeps them committed or open to switching.

## About this survey

This survey measures overall brand loyalty alongside respondents' main smartphone and computer platforms, the ecosystems they are invested in, and the brands they own multiple products from. It captures how long people have stayed with a brand, what keeps them loyal, what could make them switch, and how much brand outweighs specs and price. Likert agreement and per-attribute satisfaction batteries rate lock-in, ecosystem integration, trust, identification, and satisfaction with quality, value, support, updates, and integration, while a ranking item surfaces the top loyalty drivers. Question types include single-choice, multi-select, several Likert and satisfaction scales, split matrix batteries, a top-3 ranking, numeric and open free-text, plus overall satisfaction, recommendation, and retention-intent items, followed by a full demographic block covering age, gender, race/ethnicity, education, employment, tech-consumer type, income, household, relationship status, region, and area type.
