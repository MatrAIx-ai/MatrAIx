# Apple News

Apple News is the news app on your iPhone. It gathers stories from publishers
you care about, plus magazines and other reading you can skim through in one place.

Down at the bottom you’ll usually find a few main sections:

- **Today** — what’s happening now and stories picked for you
- **News+** — magazines, papers, and other catalog-style browsing
- **Audio** — stories you can listen to
- **Following** — the channels and topics you keep up with
- **Search** — when you want something specific

Inside each section you’ll see feeds, covers, topics, and publisher pages. Some
parts of the app may nudge you toward Apple’s paid News+ access — if you see
pricing or a signup button, take that from the screen itself, not from memory.
