# Cryptocurrency Trading Behavior

How active cryptocurrency traders behave — their trading frequency, styles, tools, risk management, and the psychological patterns that shape their decisions.

## About this survey

This survey screens for active crypto traders, then measures trading frequency, platforms, products (spot, margin, futures, options), trading style, capital deployed, and use of leverage and stop-losses. A top-3 ranking captures motivations for trading rather than holding, agreement scales probe discipline and gambling-like attitudes, and a frequency battery measures behaviors such as FOMO, panic selling, stress, and losing sleep. Later sections cover results over the past year, record-keeping, time spent, information sources, self-limiting actions, confidence, satisfaction, future intent, and a likelihood-to-recommend scale, with numeric free-text for capital and monthly trade counts. A comprehensive demographics block records age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, community type, risk tolerance, and whether the respondent trades other assets.
