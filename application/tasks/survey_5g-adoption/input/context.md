# 5G Adoption

How consumers adopt, use, and feel about 5G mobile networks and 5G-based home internet.

## About this survey

This survey begins with a screener on whether the respondent has a 5G phone and plan, how long they have used 5G, and how they came to it, then captures specifics such as their carrier, phone brand, 5G band type, the devices they connect, and the activities they perform on 5G. It measures attitudes through an agreement battery (speed, coverage, battery drain, value, and hype), an importance matrix for carrier-choice factors, and a top-three ranking of reasons for valuing 5G, alongside questions on problems experienced, battery/data management, health concerns, and 5G home internet. Outcome items cover satisfaction, likelihood to recommend, and upgrade and cord-cutting intent, using single-choice, multi-select, Likert, matrix, ranking, and numeric free-text formats. The demographic block records age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, relationship status, region, urban/suburban/rural setting, tech-adoption profile, and daily smartphone use.
