# PUR Gum Gift Suitability Survey

We're gathering reactions to **PUR Gum** — an Amazon grocery listing rated 4.5 stars across ~30,415 customer ratings.

Think of it as a possible gift for someone else. One thing to weigh: the listed price recently changed from **$1.49** to **$1.86**. After reading the brief, tell us whether you'd give it as a gift, to whom, and for what occasion.

## How to answer

- Read the brief before you start.
- Answer every required question.
- For multiple-choice, use the listed option ids.
- For rating scales, use a whole number in the given range.
- Give the answer alone unless a question also asks for a short reason or confidence.
