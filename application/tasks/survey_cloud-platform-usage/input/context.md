# Cloud Platform Usage

How developers and teams adopt and use public cloud platforms such as AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud.

## About this survey

This survey profiles cloud platform usage, beginning with a screener on whether and how long the respondent uses the cloud and their role in cloud decisions. It records the primary and secondary providers, the categories of services used, deployment model, provisioning methods, spend, cost-management practices, learning path, and certifications. Attitudes are captured with an agreement Likert battery on cost, reliability, lock-in, and security and a per-item importance matrix over provider-selection criteria, plus a top-three ranking of reasons for using the cloud and a multi-select of concerns. Additional sections address surprise costs, outage impact, containers and serverless usage, compliance requirements, and satisfaction with reliability and cost, followed by overall satisfaction, recommendation likelihood, growth intent, and switching intent. Question types include single-choice, multi-select, Likert scales, an importance matrix, a ranking item, and numeric free-text. The survey ends with a demographic block covering role, professional experience, employment, company size, industry, age, gender, race and ethnicity, education, U.S. region, and urbanicity.
