# Credit Card Rewards

How people earn, choose, and redeem credit card rewards.

## About this survey

This survey examines how many rewards cards respondents hold and from which issuers — Chase, American Express, Capital One, Citi, Discover, and store or co-branded cards — along with reward type, annual fees, how cards were chosen, and how balances are paid. It measures category optimization, redemption methods, sign-up-bonus behavior, and estimated annual rewards value. Likert batteries capture attitudes toward effort, annual fees, overspending, and paying in full, plus the importance of bonuses, fees, earn rates, travel perks, and redemption flexibility, while multi-select and top-3 items cover issuers used, spending categories, information sources, and reasons for using rewards cards. Numeric free-text records monthly charges and annual fees, and a full demographic block captures age, gender, race or ethnicity, income, education, employment, self-reported credit score range, household size, marital status, region, community type, and homeownership.
