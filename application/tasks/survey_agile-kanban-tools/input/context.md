# Agile & Kanban Tools

How software and product teams practice Agile and Kanban and the tools they use to run sprints, boards, and ceremonies.

## About this survey

This survey covers Agile and Kanban adoption and the tools that support it, such as Jira, Azure DevOps, Linear, GitHub Projects, and Trello. It measures framework choice (Scrum, Kanban, Scrumban, scaled), sprint length, ceremonies practiced, board and workflow features, estimation methods, team role and size, update frequency, and use of AI features. Attitude batteries capture agreement about predictability, tool accuracy, and ceremony value, plus a usefulness rating of individual Agile practices and a top-three tool-selection ranking. Later sections cover frustrations, process maturity, satisfaction, likelihood to recommend, tool-change intent, and time spent in ceremonies. Demographics captured include age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, years of experience, industry, organization size, region, and area type.
