# Smartphone Usage Habits

How people use their smartphones day to day, and how they feel about the amount and impact of that use.

## About this survey

This survey measures everyday smartphone behavior: platform, daily hours and pickups, morning routines, and the primary activities and app categories respondents rely on. It captures where and when they use their phones, mealtime and bedtime habits, and self-perceived overuse, then uses Likert batteries on attachment, productivity, sleep, and connection plus a frequency matrix across common activities like social media, messaging, short video, work email, gaming, and photos. Further sections cover screen-time management tools, notification handling, distraction, digital detox experience, bedtime phone placement, a top-3 values ranking, and outcome measures for satisfaction, self-rated habit health, and intent to cut back. A comprehensive demographic block captures age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, children in household, region, urbanicity, and tech comfort.
