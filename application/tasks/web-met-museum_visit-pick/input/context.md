# Task context

Browse the Metropolitan Museum of Art's public site at
https://www.metmuseum.org/.

Imagine you are planning a visit in the next few weeks and only have time to
prioritize **one thing**: a current exhibition or a specific artwork from the
collection. Explore as yourself — the exhibitions listing and the online
collection search are both public — and pick the one you would genuinely make
time for.

Read exhibition and artwork titles from the live pages; do not invent values.
No account or ticket purchase is needed to browse.
