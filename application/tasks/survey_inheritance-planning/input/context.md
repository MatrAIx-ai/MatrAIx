# Inheritance Planning

How people plan to pass on wealth, their estate-planning readiness, and their expectations and feelings about inheritance.

## About this survey

This survey screens how much thought respondents have given to passing on their assets and which estate-planning documents they hold. It captures whether they expect to leave or receive an inheritance, who and what they plan to pass on, estimated estate value, and whether they have discussed plans with family. Attitudes are measured through agreement batteries on legacy, spending versus leaving, family discomfort, and equal treatment, and a concern battery covering taxes, family conflict, long-term-care needs, probate, and heir dependence. Additional items cover professionals consulted, barriers to planning, preferred use of an inheritance, a top-3 planning-goals ranking, review frequency, tax knowledge, confidence, satisfaction, planning intent, a 0-10 preparedness scale, and an open comment field. It closes with a comprehensive demographic block including age, gender, race or ethnicity, income, net worth, education, employment, household size, marital status, whether they have children, region, community type, and homeownership.
