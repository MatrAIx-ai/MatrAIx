# Project Management Tools

How individuals and teams use project management tools to plan, track, and report on work — which tools they choose, how they use them, and how satisfied they are.

## About this survey

This survey covers adoption and use of project management platforms such as Jira, Asana, Monday.com, Trello, ClickUp, and Notion. It measures how long and how often people use these tools, how many projects they track, who selected the tool, which views and features they rely on, team size, mobile use, and spending. Attitude batteries capture agreement about organization, ease of use, and visibility, an importance rating of tool-selection factors, and a top-three priority ranking. Later sections cover frustrations, switching history, learning methods, satisfaction, likelihood to recommend, and switching intent. Demographics captured include age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, role, years of experience, organization size, region, and area type.
