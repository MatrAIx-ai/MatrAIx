# Telehealth Adoption

Whether and how people adopt and use virtual (phone or video) medical visits.

## About this survey

This survey measures whether respondents have adopted telehealth, how often they use it, and the platforms and devices they rely on. It covers the situations they use it for, motivations such as convenience and cost, and concerns such as privacy and physical examination limits. Attitudes are captured through agreement, importance, frequency, comfort, and likelihood scales, including a matrix battery on telehealth perceptions, a top-3 situations ranking, and a 0-to-10 recommendation score. Numeric and open-text items capture visit counts and adoption drivers, followed by a full demographic block including insurance status.
