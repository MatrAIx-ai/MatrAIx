# Credit Card Debt

How people carry, manage, and pay down revolving credit card debt.

## About this survey

This survey looks at whether respondents carry a balance, on how many cards, for how long, and what caused the debt, along with their payment habits, APR, and how well they know their balance. It measures payoff strategies — avalanche, snowball, balance transfers, consolidation, and credit counseling — missed payments, expected payoff timeline, and how the debt has affected savings, credit, and relationships. Likert batteries capture stress, having a plan, comfort discussing debt, and worry frequency, while multi-select and top-3 items cover causes, management actions, spillover effects, goals, and advice sources. A 0–10 control scale and numeric free-text record total debt, monthly payments, and take-home income, and a full demographic block captures age, gender, race or ethnicity, income, education, employment, self-reported credit score range, household size, marital status, region, community type, and homeownership.
