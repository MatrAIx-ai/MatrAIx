# Meditation and Mindfulness App Usage

Consumer adoption of, usage patterns for, and attitudes toward meditation and mindfulness apps.

## About this survey

This survey screens for current or past meditation-app use and tenure, then identifies which apps people have tried (Calm, Headspace, Insight Timer, Balance, and others), their primary app, and how often, how long, and when they use it. It captures the reasons people started, the features they rely on, subscription type, and annual spending. Attitudinal Likert batteries measure perceived effects on stress, sleep, focus, routine fit, and value for money, and a rated matrix battery scores helpfulness across stress, sleep, focus, mood, and habit-building. Later sections cover barriers to regular use, a top-3 ranking of features that matter when choosing an app, satisfaction, renewal likelihood, a 0–10 recommendation score, and an optional open comment. It uses single-choice, multi-select, Likert and helpfulness scales, a matrix battery, and numeric free-text, and closes with demographics covering age, gender, race and ethnicity, education, employment, relationship status, household size, region, urbanicity, device ownership, and income.
