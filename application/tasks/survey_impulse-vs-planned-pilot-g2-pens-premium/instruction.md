# Pilot G2 Pens Impulse vs Planned Survey

We're gathering reactions to **Pilot G2 Pens** — an Amazon office products listing rated 4.8 stars across ~54,512 customer ratings.

One thing to weigh: the listed price recently changed from **$12.59** to **$15.74**. After reading the brief, tell us whether buying it would be a grab-it-now impulse or a planned decision, and what would trigger the impulse.

## How to answer

- Read the brief before you start.
- Answer every required question.
- For multiple-choice, use the listed option ids.
- For rating scales, use a whole number in the given range.
- Give the answer alone unless a question also asks for a short reason or confidence.
