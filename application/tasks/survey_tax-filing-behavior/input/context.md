# Tax Filing Behavior

How individuals prepare and file their income taxes, and their attitudes toward the process.

## About this survey

This survey asks respondents how they file their federal (and state) taxes, how complex their situation is, and what applied to their most recent return, including refunds, amounts owed, deductions, and credits. Likert batteries measure stress, understanding, confidence in accuracy, and audit worry, while a priorities question ranks what matters most when filing. Additional sections cover filing timing, refund use, tax-planning behavior, IRS interactions, cost to file, where respondents seek help, satisfaction, and intentions for next year. A comprehensive demographic block including main income source supports persona matching.
