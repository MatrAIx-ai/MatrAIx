# Brokerage App Usage

How individual investors use mobile and online brokerage apps to invest and trade.

## About this survey

This survey covers brokerage app usage from platform choice (Robinhood, Fidelity, Schwab, E*TRADE, Webull, Vanguard, Cash App Investing, Public) through tenure, login and trading frequency, what respondents trade, portfolio size, monthly deposits, funding method, features used, and typical order types. It measures how apps were chosen and used, then applies agreement and importance Likert batteries to gauge accessibility, over-trading, confidence, security trust, and social-media influence. Sub-topics include investing style, options and margin use, and how the app changed overall investing, followed by confidence, likelihood of continued use, satisfaction, a 0-to-10 recommendation score, a top-3 features ranking, and an optional comment. A full demographic block records age, gender, race or ethnicity, household income, education, employment, household size, marital status, U.S. region, urban/suburban/rural setting, and self-rated investing experience.
