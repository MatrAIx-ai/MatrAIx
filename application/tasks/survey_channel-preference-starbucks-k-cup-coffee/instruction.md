# Starbucks K-Cup Coffee Pods Where-to-Buy Survey

We're gathering reactions to **Starbucks K-Cup Coffee Pods** — an Amazon grocery listing rated 4.7 stars across ~40,617 customer ratings.

One thing to weigh: the listed size recently changed from **24 Count (Pack of 4)** to **16 Count (Pack of 4)**. After reading the brief, tell us where you'd actually want to buy it and what matters about that choice.

## How to answer

- Read the brief before you start.
- Answer every required question.
- For multiple-choice, use the listed option ids.
- For rating scales, use a whole number in the given range.
- Give the answer alone unless a question also asks for a short reason or confidence.
