# Password Manager Usage

How people store, generate, and manage their online passwords, and their attitudes toward dedicated password manager tools.

## About this survey

This survey explores how respondents keep track of passwords, from dedicated managers to browser keychains and paper notes, and which products they rely on (1Password, Bitwarden, LastPass, Dashlane, Apple iCloud Keychain, Google Password Manager, and others). It measures adoption history, plan type and spending, devices and features used, password hygiene, and recovery practices, along with Likert batteries on trust, security, and convenience and an importance matrix on selection criteria. It also drills into concerns, breaches, passkey adoption, sharing behavior, a top-3 reasons ranking, and outcome measures for satisfaction, likelihood to recommend, and future intent. A comprehensive demographic block captures age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, urbanicity, tech comfort, and relationship to tech work.
