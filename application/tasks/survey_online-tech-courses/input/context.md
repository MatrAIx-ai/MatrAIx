# Online Tech Courses & E-Learning

How people take online technology courses, which platforms they use, and how they value e-learning for tech skills.

## About this survey

This survey measures participation in online technology courses, including platforms used, topics studied, motivations, formats preferred, and pricing models. It covers behaviors around completion, drop-off reasons, weekly study time, certificates earned, course discovery, devices, and supplementary resources. It uses agreement-scale and importance-scale Likert batteries, a per-item importance matrix on how respondents choose a course, a "top 3 factors" ranking, and numeric free-text items on courses completed and money spent. It closes with satisfaction, future intent, a 0-10 recommendation scale, and a full demographics block including age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, household, region, locale, current role, and tech skill.
