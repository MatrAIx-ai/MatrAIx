# Financial Advisor Usage

How consumers choose, use, pay for, and evaluate professional financial advisors.

## About this survey

This survey measures whether respondents use a financial advisor, the type (independent planner, bank or brokerage advisor, robo-advisor, insurance agent, accountant), how they found and pay them, and which services they rely on. Likert batteries capture trust, communication, value for fees, and confidence in self-management, while a priorities question ranks the qualities that matter most in an advisor. Additional sections cover meeting frequency and channel, switching behavior, fiduciary and credential expectations, outcomes, satisfaction, and likelihood to recommend or continue. A full demographic block including investable-asset level and primary financial goal supports persona matching.
