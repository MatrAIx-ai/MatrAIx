# OpenBB Finance Assistant

Your portfolio manager asks you before tomorrow's meeting:
**"Is NVIDIA (NVDA) still worth its price?"**

Use the research assistant to answer. It has live market data and answers
with numbers. Get three things, in your own words, one ask per message:

1. **The picture now.** NVDA's current price, how it has moved recently,
   and any news driving it.
2. **The price tag.** NVDA's P/E, forward P/E, and revenue growth. If a
   number surprises you, ask a follow-up about it.
3. **The comparison.** The same metrics for one peer of your choice
   (for example AMD or MSFT) and for the S&P 500. Challenge at least one
   figure: ask where it comes from or how current it is.

Then give your answer: **overweight, neutral, or avoid**, backed by the
two or three numbers that decide it for you. Ask the assistant whether it
agrees with your call, and end the conversation after its reply.

If an answer is vague or dodges the number you asked for, push back and
re-ask before moving on.
