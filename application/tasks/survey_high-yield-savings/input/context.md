# High-Yield Savings Accounts

How people use high-yield savings accounts to earn interest on their cash.

## About this survey

This survey explores whether respondents hold a high-yield savings account (HYSA), which provider they use — Ally, Marcus by Goldman Sachs, SoFi, American Express, Capital One, Discover, or others — the APY they earn, and what they save toward. It measures how accounts were discovered and chosen, deposit and withdrawal habits, rate-comparison behavior, transfer speed, and use of related products like CDs, money market accounts, and Treasuries. Likert batteries capture attitudes toward online banks and rate-chasing, plus the importance of APY, fees, insurance, access, and brand, while multi-select and top-3 items cover goals, discovery channels, and selection factors. Numeric free-text records balance, monthly deposits, and household income, and a comprehensive demographic block captures age, gender, race or ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, community type, and homeownership.
