# Personal Budget Coach Chatbot

You take home about **$3,200 a month** and pay **$1,400 in rent**, but the money is gone by
the end of the month and you can't see where. You want to start saving **$300 every month**.

Your goal: leave the chat with a **concrete monthly budget split** and **at least one
specific place to cut** that gets you to your $300 savings target.

- Share your income, rent, and that you eat out often / have several subscriptions when the
  assistant asks.
- Ask for actual dollar targets per category, not just general advice.

Chat as yourself — the assigned persona. Keep going until you have a plan you could actually
follow (or conclude the assistant couldn't give you one).
