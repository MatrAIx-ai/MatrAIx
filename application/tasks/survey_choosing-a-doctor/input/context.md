# Choosing a Doctor

How people research, evaluate, and decide which doctor to see.

## About this survey

This survey explores what prompts people to seek a new doctor and which sources and characteristics they rely on to evaluate options. It measures use of online reviews, insurance directories, and personal recommendations, along with the difficulty of finding available providers. Attitudes are captured through importance, agreement, frequency, satisfaction, and likelihood scales, including an importance matrix battery, a top-3 factor ranking, and a 0-to-10 recommendation score. Numeric and open-text items capture comparison behavior and the qualities respondents value most, followed by a comprehensive demographic block including insurance status.
