# Buy Now, Pay Later (BNPL)

How consumers adopt, use, and feel about buy now, pay later installment services.

## About this survey

This survey covers BNPL usage from adoption and frequency through the specific providers used (Affirm, Klarna, Afterpay, PayPal Pay in 4, Zip, Sezzle), where and for what categories purchases are split, purchase amounts, number of active plans, and repayment structure. It measures behaviors such as how plans are paid, missed payments, and late fees, then uses agreement and importance Likert batteries to capture attitudes about affordability, overspending, term understanding, and repayment worry. Sub-topics include purchase regret, changing usage over time, budgeting, and the counterfactual payment method, followed by likelihood of future use, satisfaction, a 0-to-10 recommendation score, a top-3 reasons ranking, a confidence rating, and an optional comment. A full demographic block records age, gender, race or ethnicity, household income, education, employment, household size, marital status, U.S. region, urban/suburban/rural setting, and credit card ownership.
