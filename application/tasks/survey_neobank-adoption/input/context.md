# Neobank Adoption

Adoption and use of neobanks and digital-only banking apps such as Chime, SoFi, Cash App, Varo, and Current.

## About this survey

This survey screens for awareness and use of neobanks, tenure, and which apps respondents use, then explores what first drew them in, their primary use case, and the features they value most (no fees, early direct deposit, high-yield savings, round-ups, notifications, ATM access). Free-text items capture the share of spending routed through the neobank and estimated annual savings. Likert batteries measure attitudes about cost savings, app quality, trust and safety, the branch-less model, and customer-service worries, plus an importance matrix on choosing a neobank (fees, savings rate, early deposit, FDIC insurance, app quality) and a top-3 ranking of reasons for use. Additional items cover concerns, FDIC-insurance awareness, past problems, deposit methods, fee comparisons, likelihood to make it primary, satisfaction, a 0–10 recommendation score, and an optional comment. A comprehensive demographic block records age, gender, race/ethnicity, education, employment, income, marital status, household size, region, urbanicity, and comfort with financial technology.
