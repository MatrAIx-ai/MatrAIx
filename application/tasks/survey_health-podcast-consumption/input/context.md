# Health Podcast Consumption

How people listen to, engage with, and act on health and wellness podcasts.

## About this survey

This survey examines consumers' consumption of health, wellness, fitness, and medical podcasts. It opens with screener questions on podcast listening habits and tenure, then measures behaviors such as preferred platforms (Spotify, Apple Podcasts, YouTube), listening contexts and devices, genres followed, discovery methods, and weekly listening time. Likert batteries capture attitudes toward learning, motivation, trust, and misinformation, an importance battery rates what matters when choosing a show, and a top-3 ranking identifies listening motivations. Additional questions cover behavior change, advertising tolerance, fact-checking, paid subscriptions, satisfaction, likelihood to recommend, and future intent. A full demographic block captures age, gender, race and ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, U.S. region, community type, and overall interest in health topics. Question types include single-choice, multi-select, several rating scales, a matrix battery, a ranking item, and numeric free-text.
