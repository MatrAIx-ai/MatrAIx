# education assistant

An AI tutoring chatbot that helps learners understand academic concepts through adaptive dialogue.

It asks about your current knowledge level and learning goals, then tailors explanations to your needs.

What it is good for: getting step-by-step explanations, breaking down complex topics, and filling knowledge gaps.

What it is not: a homework solver, a grading tool, or a substitute for a human teacher.

## What you can do in this product

- Ask questions about a topic and get explanations at your level.
- Answer the chatbot's clarifying questions about what you already know.
