# Mobile App Subscriptions

How people sign up for, manage, pay for, and cancel app and digital subscriptions.

## About this survey

This survey examines subscription behavior: how many paid subscriptions respondents hold, which categories (video, music, cloud storage, news, fitness, productivity, gaming, dating, learning, and AI tools), and total monthly spending. It measures how people start subscriptions, preferred payment methods, free-trial habits, tracking methods, and cancellation frequency and triggers, then uses Likert batteries on unused subscriptions, value, cancellation friction, and one-time-purchase preference plus an importance matrix on what drives keeping a subscription. Additional sections cover family sharing, surprise charges, annual-plan use, a top-3 retention-factors ranking, and outcome measures for management satisfaction, perceived spending control, and cancellation intent. The demographic block captures age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, urbanicity, money-management style, and mobile platform.
