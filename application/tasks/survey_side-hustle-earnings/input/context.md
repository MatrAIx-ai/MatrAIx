# Side Hustle Earnings

How people run, earn from, and feel about a side hustle alongside their main income.

## About this survey

This survey asks respondents what kind of side hustle they run (online sales, freelancing, content creation, tutoring, renting, reselling), how long they've done it, weekly hours, monthly earnings, and startup investment. Numeric questions capture hours and dollars, Likert batteries measure importance, growth potential, work-life balance, and profitability, and a priorities question ranks why the side hustle matters most. Additional sections cover how they find customers, handle taxes and separate money, biggest challenges, confidence, satisfaction, and intentions to continue or scale up. A comprehensive demographic block including primary-job status supports persona matching.
