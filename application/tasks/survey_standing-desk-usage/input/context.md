# Standing Desk Usage

How people use standing desks and manage sitting time during their workday.

## About this survey

This survey explores how people use standing and sit-stand desks and manage sitting during work. It opens with screener questions on usage, location, tenure, desk type, and brand (Uplift, FlexiSpot, Vari, and others), then measures behaviors including hours spent sitting versus standing, how often people switch positions, accessories used, and reasons for adopting a standing desk. Likert batteries capture perceived comfort, energy, focus, and value, change items rate how back comfort, energy, and productivity have shifted, and an importance battery rates desk features. A top-3 ranking identifies the benefits people value most, and further questions cover reminders, other anti-sitting habits, spending, satisfaction, likelihood to recommend, and future intent. A comprehensive demographic block captures age, gender, race and ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, U.S. region, community type, work arrangement, and nature of work. Question types include single-choice, multi-select, several rating scales, a matrix battery, a ranking item, and numeric free-text.
