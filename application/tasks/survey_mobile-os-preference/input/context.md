# Mobile Operating System Preference

People's preferences, loyalty, and switching behavior between iPhone (iOS) and Android.

## About this survey

This survey captures which mobile platform and phone brand respondents use, how long they have used it, and whether they have switched between iOS and Android. It measures how they chose their platform, which ecosystem devices they own, how locked in they feel, and how much they paid for their phone, then uses Likert batteries on usability, customization, privacy, and brand loyalty, a satisfaction matrix across dimensions like apps, camera, updates, battery, and integration, and an importance matrix on selection criteria. Additional sections cover likes, frustrations, cross-platform friction, a top-3 loyalty-factors ranking, and outcome measures for satisfaction, likelihood to recommend, and switching intent. The demographic block captures age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, urbanicity, and tech comfort.
