# Auto Loan & Vehicle Financing

How consumers shop for, take on, and manage auto loans and vehicle leases.

## About this survey

This survey covers the full arc of vehicle financing, from acquisition history and current loan or lease status through the specifics of the most recent loan — amount financed, term, APR, monthly payment, down payment, lender source, add-on products, and credit tier. It measures shopping behavior (pre-approval, comparing offers), attitudes through agreement and importance Likert batteries about the borrowing experience, and sub-topics such as refinancing, being underwater, and payment reliability. Later questions capture satisfaction, likelihood to finance again or go electric, a 0-to-10 recommendation score, a top-3 priorities ranking, and an optional open comment. A comprehensive demographic block records age, gender, race or ethnicity, household income, education, employment, household size, marital status, U.S. region, urban/suburban/rural setting, and homeownership.
