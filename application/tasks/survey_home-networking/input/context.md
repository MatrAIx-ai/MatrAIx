# Home Networking

How households set up, manage, secure, and troubleshoot their home network and Wi-Fi, and how satisfied they are with the equipment.

## About this survey

This survey profiles who manages the home network, the router or mesh setup, brands and Wi-Fi standards in use, wired Ethernet, and the range of connected devices. It measures how people manage settings, which security practices they follow, firmware-update habits, coverage-boosting equipment, parental controls, and how they troubleshoot problems. Likert agreement and per-attribute satisfaction batteries gauge understanding, coverage, ease of management, security worry, and satisfaction with coverage, speed, setup, reliability, and security, while a ranking item surfaces the top features people buy on. Question types include single-choice, multi-select, several Likert and satisfaction scales, split matrix batteries, a top-3 ranking, numeric and open free-text, plus satisfaction, recommendation, and upgrade-intent items, followed by a full demographic block covering age, gender, race/ethnicity, education, employment, tech comfort, income, household, homeownership, region, and area type.
