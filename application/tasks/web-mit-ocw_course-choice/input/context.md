# Task context

Browse the public course catalog at https://ocw.mit.edu/.

You are deciding on the **one course** you would most realistically start
learning from in the next month. Explore as yourself: use search or the topic
browse pages, open a few course pages, and weigh what actually matters to you —
your learning goal, how advanced the material is, whether it has lecture videos
or just notes, and how much time you can commit.

Read the course title and course number from the live page; do not invent
values. Everything on OCW is free and open; no account is needed.
