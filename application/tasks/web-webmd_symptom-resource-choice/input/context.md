# Task context

You have a health concern or symptom you want to understand better before
deciding what to do next. You are not looking for a diagnosis from a website —
you want clear, credible information that helps you think through what you are
experiencing and what sensible next steps might be.

Browse the public WebMD site at https://www.webmd.com/ as yourself. Let your
background, health history, risk posture, reading preferences, and how cautious
or urgent the concern feels shape what you search for and which articles you
take seriously.

Use only information visible on the live WebMD site. Do not invent symptoms,
diagnoses, test results, or clinician recommendations that are not part of the
information provided about you or the pages you read.
