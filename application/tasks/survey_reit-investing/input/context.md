# REIT Investing

How people invest in real estate investment trusts (REITs) and REIT funds.

## About this survey

This survey screens for REIT ownership and familiarity and captures how long respondents have invested, how they hold REITs (individual publicly traded REITs, ETFs like VNQ and SCHH, mutual funds, non-traded REITs, retirement accounts, crowdfunding), and which property sectors they invest in. It records portfolio share, amount invested, dividend income, reasons for investing, and dividend reinvestment. Agreement and importance batteries measure attitudes on passive income, convenience, volatility, diversification, understanding, and interest-rate sensitivity, including a matrix of selection criteria and a top-3 ranking of decision factors. Additional items cover interest-rate concern, tax awareness, information sources, risk tolerance, monitoring frequency, and confidence. It closes with changing allocation, satisfaction, forward plans, a likelihood-to- recommend score, and an open comment. A comprehensive demographic block records age, gender, race/ethnicity, household income, education, employment, total investable assets, household size, marital status, region, and area type so personas can be matched.
