# Debt Management Strategies

How consumers carry, prioritize, and pay down their debts, and how they feel about it.

## About this survey

This survey asks respondents about the types and amount of debt they carry, whether it is rising or falling, and which payoff strategies they use (snowball, avalanche, balance transfers, consolidation, credit counseling, or minimum payments). Likert batteries measure stress, sense of control, clarity of plans, and understanding of interest rates, while a priorities question ranks what matters most in tackling debt. Additional sections cover missed payments, tools used, consolidation and counseling experiences, bankruptcy consideration, information sources, confidence, satisfaction with progress, and likelihood of new borrowing. A comprehensive demographic block including overall debt situation supports persona matching.
