# CRM Software Usage

How people and teams use customer relationship management (CRM) software to manage contacts, leads, and sales pipelines — which platforms they choose, how they use them, and how satisfied they are.

## About this survey

This survey covers use of CRM platforms such as Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho CRM, Microsoft Dynamics 365, and Pipedrive. It measures how long people have used a CRM, which platforms and functions they use, their role, update frequency, database size, data-entry and integration methods, AI features, and mobile use. Attitude batteries capture agreement about effectiveness, ease of use, data quality, and visibility, plus a performance rating across ease, reporting, integrations, customization, and value, and a top-three tool-selection ranking. Later sections cover frustrations, team adoption, cost, switching history, satisfaction, likelihood to recommend, and switching intent. Demographics captured include age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, years of experience, industry, organization size, region, and area type.
