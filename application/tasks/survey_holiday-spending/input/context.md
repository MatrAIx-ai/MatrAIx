# Holiday Spending

How people budget for and spend money during the winter holiday season — on gifts, travel, food, and celebrations.

## About this survey

This survey captures total planned holiday spending and its breakdown across gifts, travel, food, decorations, and charity, along with the number of gift recipients, payment methods, shopping venues, timing, and whether the respondent sets a budget. Likert batteries measure spending pressure, financial strain, overspending tendencies, the link between spending and affection, and planning habits; an importance battery rates gift-buying criteria; and a "top 3" ranking identifies budget priorities. Additional questions cover deal usage, year-over-year and inflation-driven changes, holiday debt and payoff time, spending stress, enjoyment, regret, satisfaction with money management, and intent to save earlier. It closes with a comprehensive demographic block (age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, children, region, and urban/rural).
