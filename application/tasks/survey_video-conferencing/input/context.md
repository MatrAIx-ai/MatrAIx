# Video Conferencing

How people use video conferencing tools for meetings and calls — which tools they choose, how they run meetings, and how video calls affect their workday.

## About this survey

This survey covers use of video conferencing platforms such as Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, and Webex. It measures how long and how often people meet by video, which tools and join methods they use, meeting purposes, camera habits, features used, AI meeting tools, and equipment. Attitude batteries capture agreement about effectiveness, meeting fatigue, and quality, plus a frequency battery on call problems and distractions and an importance rating of tool-selection factors with a top-three ranking. Later sections cover frustrations, payment, satisfaction, likelihood to recommend, and expected change in meeting time. Demographics captured include age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, work arrangement, role, region, and area type.
