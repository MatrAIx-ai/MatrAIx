# Email Newsletter Management

How people manage their email inboxes, newsletter subscriptions, and promotional mail.

## About this survey

This survey begins with a screener on inbox state, number of newsletter subscriptions and email accounts, and checking frequency, then details the providers and apps used, how and where newsletters are read, the topics subscribed to, and the tactics used to manage and unsubscribe from mail. Attitudes are measured with an agreement battery (control, newsletter overload, read-through, unsubscribe hassle, informational value, overload stress, and unread guilt), an inbox-philosophy item, an importance matrix for email features, and a top-three ranking of reasons to subscribe, plus unsubscribe triggers, alias use, and clean-up recency. Outcome items cover satisfaction, sense of control, decluttering intent, and likelihood to recommend an email provider, using single-choice, multi-select, Likert, matrix, ranking, and numeric free-text formats. The demographic block records age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, relationship status, region, urban/suburban/rural setting, reliance on email for work, and overall tech comfort.
