# Team Chat Tools

How people use workplace team chat and messaging tools — which tools they choose, what they use them for, and how chat affects focus, connection, and productivity.

## About this survey

This survey covers use of workplace messaging platforms such as Slack, Microsoft Teams, Discord, and Google Chat. It measures how long and how many hours people use these tools, which tools and access methods they use, who chose the tool, the purposes and features they rely on, channel membership, notification management, AI features, and external communication. Attitude batteries capture agreement about speed, response pressure, interruptions, and connection, plus a frequency battery on overload, missed messages, and after-hours checking, and a top-three tool-selection ranking. Later sections cover frustrations, payment, satisfaction, likelihood to recommend, and switching intent. Demographics captured include age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, work arrangement, role, organization size, region, and area type.
