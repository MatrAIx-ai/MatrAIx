# Cryptocurrency Ownership

Who owns cryptocurrency and digital assets, how they hold and manage them, and their attitudes toward the risks and future of crypto.

## About this survey

This survey begins by screening for current or past cryptocurrency ownership, then captures holdings (Bitcoin, Ethereum, stablecoins, altcoins, ETFs), storage methods, platforms, holding value, and portfolio share. It measures motivations through a top-3 ranking, behaviors around buying and activities like staking and NFTs, and attitudes via agreement scales and a concern battery covering volatility, key loss, exchange failure, fraud, and taxes. Later sections address security confidence, past losses, information sources, tax handling, satisfaction, future buying intent, and a likelihood-to-recommend scale, with numeric free-text for holding value and monthly contributions. A full demographics block records age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, community type, fintech comfort, and risk tolerance.
