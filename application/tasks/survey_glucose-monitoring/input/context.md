# Glucose Monitoring

Consumer awareness of, attitudes toward, and interest in glucose monitoring for wellness.

## About this survey

This survey explores consumer attitudes toward and interest in glucose monitoring for general wellness, including continuous glucose monitors from brands like Dexcom Stelo, Abbott Lingo, Levels, and Nutrisense. It opens with screener questions on familiarity, past use, device type, and brand awareness, then measures wellness goals, information sources, and how much people think about blood sugar and energy. Likert batteries capture attitudes toward understanding the body, food choices, appeal, cost, and trust, while an importance battery rates factors in choosing a monitor. A top-3 ranking identifies what people most want from glucose data, and further questions cover willingness to wear a sensor, willingness to pay, current diet and tracking habits, privacy concerns, barriers, interest, and likelihood to recommend. A comprehensive demographic block captures age, gender, race and ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, U.S. region, community type, nutrition interest, and insurance status. Question types include single-choice, multi-select, several rating scales, a matrix battery, a ranking item, and numeric free-text.
