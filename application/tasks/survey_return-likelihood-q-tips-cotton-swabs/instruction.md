# Q-tips Cotton Swabs Return Likelihood Survey

We're gathering reactions to **Q-tips Cotton Swabs** — an Amazon beauty listing rated 4.9 stars across ~18,720 customer ratings.

Imagine you bought it and it just arrived. One thing to weigh: the listed size recently changed from **750 Count (Pack of 1)** to **500 Count (Pack of 1)**. After reading the brief, tell us how likely you'd be to send it back, why, and what would convince you to keep it.

## How to answer

- Read the brief before you start.
- Answer every required question.
- For multiple-choice, use the listed option ids.
- For rating scales, use a whole number in the given range.
- Give the answer alone unless a question also asks for a short reason or confidence.
