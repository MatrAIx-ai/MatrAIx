# Cashback and Loyalty Programs

How consumers use cashback and loyalty rewards programs.

## About this survey

This survey covers whether and how many rewards programs people join, the types they use (cashback and travel cards, grocery and store loyalty, coffee and restaurant rewards, airline and hotel programs), and the cashback apps they rely on such as Rakuten, Ibotta, Fetch, and Upside. It measures how rewards are earned and redeemed, estimated annual value, redemption timing, and lost or expired points. Likert and influence batteries capture whether programs save money, drive overspending or brand loyalty, feel like a hassle, and raise data-privacy concerns, along with the factors that steer where people shop, plus a ranked set of most-valued program features. Additional items probe annual fees, carrying a balance, effort to maximize rewards, and changing usage. Closing items gauge confidence, satisfaction, and intent, and a full demographic block records age, gender, race and ethnicity, income, education, employment, marital status, household size, online-versus-in-store shopping, region, and urbanicity.
