# Auto Insurance Shopping

How consumers shop for, choose, and switch auto insurance, and what drives their decisions about price, coverage, and providers.

## About this survey

This survey screens for current auto insurance status and provider, then captures tenure, monthly premium, vehicles covered, shopping frequency, and the channels used to get quotes. It documents coverage types, deductibles, discounts, telematics participation, bundling, and claims history, and measures decision drivers through an importance battery covering price, reputation, service, claims, discounts, and digital tools. Attitude scales probe policy understanding, price fairness, and switching willingness, while a top-3 ranking captures switching triggers. Later sections cover premium changes, past switching, satisfaction, future shopping intent, and a likelihood-to-recommend scale, with numeric free-text for premium, vehicle count, household size, and licensed drivers. A comprehensive demographics block records age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, marital status, region, community type, and homeownership.
