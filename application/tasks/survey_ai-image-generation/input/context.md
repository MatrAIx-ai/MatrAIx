# AI Image Generation

How people use AI text-to-image and image-editing tools — which tools they choose, what they create, and how they feel about quality, cost, and ethics.

## About this survey

This survey covers adoption and use of AI image-generation tools such as Midjourney, DALL·E, Stable Diffusion, Adobe Firefly, and Canva AI. It measures which tools people use and prefer, how often and for what purposes they generate images, spending and payment models, prompting skill and techniques, and local versus cloud usage. Attitude batteries capture agreement with statements about time savings, quality, and control, along with an importance rating of factors that drive tool choice and a top-three priority ranking. Later sections cover satisfaction, likelihood to recommend, frustrations, disclosure habits, and ethical concerns about copyright and human artists, plus future-use intent. Demographics captured include age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, creative background, household size, region, and area type.
