# legal assistant

A legal information assistant focused on tenant rights and landlord-tenant law.

It asks about your housing situation and explains relevant rules for your jurisdiction.

What it is good for: understanding your rights as a tenant, learning about eviction rules, security deposits, and repair obligations.

What it is not: a licensed attorney, legal representation, or a substitute for professional legal advice.

## What you can do in this product

- Describe your housing situation and get information about relevant laws.
- Ask follow-up questions about specific tenant rights.
