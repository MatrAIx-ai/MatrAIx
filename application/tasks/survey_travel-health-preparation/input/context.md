# Travel Health Preparation

How travelers prepare for and manage their health before and during trips.

## About this survey

This survey examines how travelers prepare for staying healthy while traveling. It begins with screener questions on travel frequency, trip types, and international travel, then measures preparation behaviors such as packing a medicine kit, carrying prescriptions, researching destination risks, buying travel medical insurance, and consulting travel clinics. Confidence items rate managing minor illness and finding care away from home, Likert batteries capture attitudes and worries, and a concern battery rates specific travel health issues like food and water safety and access to care. A top-3 ranking identifies the most important preparations, and further questions cover information sources, medication handling, travel apps, past travel illness, satisfaction, and future intent. A comprehensive demographic block captures age, gender, race and ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, U.S. region, community type, medication needs, and insurance status. Question types include single-choice, multi-select, several rating scales, a matrix battery, a ranking item, and numeric free-text.
