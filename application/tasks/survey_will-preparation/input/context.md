# Will Preparation

How adults approach preparing a last will and testament.

## About this survey

This survey covers whether people have a will, when it was made, how it was prepared (attorney, online services such as LegalZoom or Trust & Will, templates, or DIY), what it cost, and whether it was properly witnessed or notarized. It measures where wills are stored, what they address (heirs, executors, guardians, specific gifts, pets, funeral wishes, digital assets), and the life events that prompt people to make or update one. Likert and importance batteries capture beliefs about who needs a will, trust in online services, worry, and the reasons wills matter, from directing inheritance to naming guardians and preventing family disputes, plus a ranked set of goals. Closing items probe review habits, family conversations, knowledge, confidence, satisfaction, and intent. A full demographic block records age, gender, race and ethnicity, income, education, employment, marital status, whether respondents have children, homeownership, region, and urbanicity.
