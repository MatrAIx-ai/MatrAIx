# Employer Health Benefits

How employees understand, use, and value the health benefits offered by their employer.

## About this survey

This survey measures what health benefits respondents' employers offer, how they enroll, and how well they understand their options. It covers premium contributions, the resources used at open enrollment, and how much benefits influence job decisions. Attitudes are captured through satisfaction, agreement, frequency, importance, and likelihood scales, including a satisfaction matrix battery, a top-3 valued-benefits ranking, and a 0-to-10 recommendation score. Numeric and open-text items capture monthly premium contributions and desired changes, followed by a full demographic block.
