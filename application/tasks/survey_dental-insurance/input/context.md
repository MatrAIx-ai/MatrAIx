# Dental Insurance

How consumers choose, use, understand, and feel about dental insurance and dental benefit plans.

## About this survey

This survey measures whether respondents have dental coverage, how they obtain it, the plan type, what it covers, and what they pay in premiums. It captures the factors that drove plan choice, understanding of benefits, Likert batteries on the value and clarity of coverage, and a matrix rating satisfaction with cost, covered services, network, out-of-pocket costs, and the claims process. It also covers in-network use, denied claims, treatments delayed due to limited coverage, how people verify benefits, importance of coverage, renewal and shopping intent, overall satisfaction, a likelihood-to-recommend scale, and a top-3 ranking of desired improvements. Question types include single-choice, multi-select, several Likert scales, a matrix battery split per item, numeric free-text items, and a demographic block covering age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, area type, dependents on the plan, and self-rated health.
