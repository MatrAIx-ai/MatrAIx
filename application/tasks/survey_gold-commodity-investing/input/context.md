# Gold and Commodity Investing

How people invest in gold, precious metals, and other commodities, and why.

## About this survey

This survey screens for ownership of gold, precious metals, and commodity investments and captures how long respondents have invested, which instruments they hold (physical metal, ETFs like GLD and SLV, mining stocks, energy and agricultural commodities, broad commodity funds), and how they store them. It records portfolio share, total invested, trading frequency, reasons for investing, and where physical metal is bought. Agreement and concern batteries measure beliefs about inflation hedging, crisis protection, tangibility, volatility, and storage costs, including a matrix of specific risks and a top-3 ranking of decision factors. Additional items cover price-tracking habits, information sources, risk tolerance, changing allocation, and confidence. It closes with satisfaction, forward-looking plans, a likelihood-to-recommend score, and an open comment. A comprehensive demographic block records age, gender, race/ethnicity, household income, education, employment, total investable assets, household size, marital status, region, and area type so personas can be matched.
