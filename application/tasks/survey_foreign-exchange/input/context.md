# Foreign Exchange and Currency

How people exchange and spend foreign currency, mainly for travel and cross-border needs.

## About this survey

This survey screens for needing foreign currency and captures the reasons (travel, business, remittances, overseas purchases, study abroad, investing) and how often respondents travel internationally. It covers how currency is obtained and how people pay abroad (cash, cards, multi-currency cards, mobile wallets), amounts exchanged, fees paid, and countries visited, plus awareness of exchange rates, foreign transaction fees, and dynamic currency conversion prompts. Agreement and importance batteries measure attitudes about fees, rate shopping, airport exchanges, card safety, and budget impact, including a matrix on what matters when exchanging and a top-3 ranking of trusted methods. Additional items cover multi-currency accounts, rate tracking, retail forex trading, leftover-currency habits, confidence, and fee concern. It closes with satisfaction, likelihood of using a travel card, and an open comment. A comprehensive demographic block records age, gender, race/ethnicity, household income, education, employment, cross-border ties, household size, marital status, region, and area type so personas can be matched.
