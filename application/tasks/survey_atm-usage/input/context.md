# ATM Usage

How people use ATMs to access cash and how they handle ATM fees.

## About this survey

This survey covers how often respondents use ATMs, which types and networks they use (own bank, out-of-network, surcharge-free networks, standalone, in-store), and what they use them for (withdrawals, deposits, balance checks, transfers). It measures typical and monthly withdrawal amounts, number of visits, sensitivity to out-of-network fees and fee-avoidance behavior, fees actually paid, bank fee reimbursement, how respondents locate ATMs, cardless access, and problems encountered. Attitudes are captured through agreement, frequency, and importance batteries, including a matrix on convenience, security, fees, and cash trends, plus a top-3 ranking of factors for choosing an ATM. It closes with satisfaction, changing usage, future intent, a likelihood-to- recommend score, and an open comment. A comprehensive demographic block captures age, gender, race/ethnicity, household income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, area type, and primary institution type so personas can be matched.
