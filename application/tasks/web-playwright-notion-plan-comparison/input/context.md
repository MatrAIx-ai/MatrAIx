# Task context

Imagine that you are choosing a Notion workspace plan for your own realistic
personal or work context.

Browse the public pricing page at https://www.notion.com/pricing as yourself.
Use the standard monthly prices and compare the Free, Plus, Business, and
Enterprise plans. Let the way you would use the workspace, the people you
would collaborate with, the features that matter to you, your spending
posture, and other relevant persona attributes shape the choice.

Do not invent an exact team size, compliance requirement, security policy, or
organizational rule that is not stated in your persona or on the page. If your
persona does not supply a work or organization context, make the choice for
your own individual use. Do not apply special discounts, promotions, or
negotiated pricing.
