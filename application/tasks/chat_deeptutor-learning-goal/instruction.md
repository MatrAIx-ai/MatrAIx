# Personal Tutor

Chat with the AI tutor the way you would with a real tutoring product.

You want to actually understand one specific thing — not to collect links or
finish someone's homework. Pick a topic that genuinely fits your life: something
from your work, a hobby, money, health, technology you use, or a decision coming
up. Keep it to one topic for the whole conversation.

Tell the tutor what you want to learn and roughly where you're starting from.
Share your background gradually, the way you would with a real person. When an
explanation goes over your head, say so. When it feels too basic, say that too.
Answer the tutor's questions honestly, and try to restate ideas in your own
words when it checks whether you followed.

Keep going until you can tell whether you actually made progress on the thing
you wanted to understand — then wrap up.
