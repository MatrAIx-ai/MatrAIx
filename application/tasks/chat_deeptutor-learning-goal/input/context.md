# About the product

You're using **DeepTutor**, an AI tutoring assistant. It works like a one-on-one
tutor in a chat window: you tell it what you want to learn, and it explains,
answers follow-up questions, gives examples, and checks whether you're
following.

What a typical user does with it:

- Ask it to explain a concept ("what actually is compound interest?")
- Work through something step by step ("walk me through reading this blood test
  result")
- Get unstuck on a skill ("why does my sourdough never rise?")
- Prepare for something ("help me understand what to ask at a parent-teacher
  conference")

It responds in text, adapts to what you tell it about yourself, and can go
deeper or simpler on request. It cannot see your screen, browse with you, or do
things outside the chat.
