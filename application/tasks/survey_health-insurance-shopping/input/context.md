# Health Insurance Shopping

How people research, compare, and choose health insurance plans.

## About this survey

This survey examines how respondents shop for health insurance, the tools and sources they use, and the plan features they compare most closely. It measures how easy the process feels, understanding of insurance terms, and confidence in the final choice. Attitudes are captured through importance, agreement, frequency, satisfaction, and likelihood scales, including an importance matrix battery, a top-3 decision-factor ranking, and a 0-to-10 recommendation score. Numeric and open-text items capture how many plans people compare and their biggest difficulties, followed by a full demographic block.
