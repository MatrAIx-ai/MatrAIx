# Tech Support Experience

People's experiences seeking and receiving technical support for their devices, software, and services.

## About this survey

This survey measures how often people contact tech support, which companies and channels they use, and where they turn first when a problem arises. It covers recent-issue details such as topic, resolution, and time to resolve, along with common frustrations, attitudes toward chatbots, self-service reliance, and whether people are the "go-to" tech helper in their circle. It uses agreement-scale and importance-scale Likert batteries, a per-item importance matrix on what makes a good support experience, a "top 3 wants" ranking, and numeric free-text items on contacts per issue and support requests per year. It closes with satisfaction, a 0-10 recommendation scale, and a full demographics block including age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, household, region, locale, and tech skill.
