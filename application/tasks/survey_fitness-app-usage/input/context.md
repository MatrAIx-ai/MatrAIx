# Fitness App Usage

Consumer adoption and use of smartphone fitness and workout apps.

## About this survey

This survey measures whether respondents use fitness apps, how long they have used them, which apps (Nike Training Club, Strava, Peloton App, Apple Fitness+, and others), the primary type they rely on, how often they open them, and how they discovered them. It captures valued features, subscription and monthly spend, fitness goals, wearable and health-app integration, completion behavior, and where workouts happen. Attitudes are measured through agreement, satisfaction, and importance batteries covering consistency, personalization, motivation, ease of use, privacy, and value, followed by barriers, churn, a likelihood-to-recommend score, a top-three improvements ranking, and future-use intent. A comprehensive demographic block captures age band, gender, race or ethnicity, household income, education, employment status, household size, marital status, U.S. region, urban/suburban/rural setting, wearable ownership, and self-rated health.
