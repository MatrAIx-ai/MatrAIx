# Gig Economy Income

How people earn, manage, and feel about income from gig-economy platforms.

## About this survey

This survey asks respondents which gig platforms they earn from (rideshare, delivery, freelance, task work, rentals, resale), how long they've done it, their weekly hours and monthly earnings, and what share of total income it represents. Numeric questions capture hours and dollars, Likert batteries measure financial security, fairness, benefits worries, and budgeting difficulty, and a priorities question ranks what workers value most. Additional sections cover taxes and expense tracking, access to benefits, managing irregular income, retirement saving, satisfaction, and future intentions. A comprehensive demographic block including whether gig work is a main or supplemental job supports persona matching.
