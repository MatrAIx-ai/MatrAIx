# Podcast App Usage

How people listen to podcasts and which apps they use, including listening habits, genres, discovery, and attitudes toward ads and paid content.

## About this survey

This survey covers podcast listening behavior: whether and how long respondents have listened, which apps they use, weekly episode counts, number of shows followed, devices, and listening contexts. It measures discovery methods and listening behaviors (speed, ad-skipping, downloads, sharing, reviewing) through a frequency battery, and attitudes toward learning value, host connection, ads, and paying for ad-free content through Likert scales. An importance battery covers desired app features, and a top-3 ranking captures favorite genres, alongside video podcasts, paid subscriptions and spend, audiobooks, app switching, and future listening intent. The question types include single-choice, multi-select, Likert and importance batteries, a ranking question, a 0–10 recommendation scale, and numeric free-text. Demographics captured include age, gender, race or ethnicity, household income, education, employment status, household size, U.S. region, and urban/suburban/rural setting.
