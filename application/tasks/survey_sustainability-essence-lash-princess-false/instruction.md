# essence Sustainability Survey

We're gathering reactions to **essence** from Essence Cosmetics — an Amazon beauty listing rated 4.3 stars across ~415,260 customer ratings.

One thing to weigh: the listed price recently changed from **$4.97** to **$6.21**. After reading the brief, tell us how much environmental and ethical impact shapes your view of it, and what would ease your concerns.

## How to answer

- Read the brief before you start.
- Answer every required question.
- For multiple-choice, use the listed option ids.
- For rating scales, use a whole number in the given range.
- Give the answer alone unless a question also asks for a short reason or confidence.
