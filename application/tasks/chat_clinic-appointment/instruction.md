# Clinic Appointment Booking Chatbot

You are overdue for your **annual physical check-up** and want to book one through the
clinic's chatbot.

Your goal: get an appointment booked and confirmed for a **weekday morning within the next
two weeks**, with **Dr. Lee** if she is available (otherwise the next available provider).

- Say why you're booking (a routine physical) and give your preferred day/time and provider
  when asked.
- Get an explicit confirmation of the date, time, and provider before you finish.

Chat as yourself — the assigned persona. Keep going until the appointment is confirmed or
you're told it can't be booked.
