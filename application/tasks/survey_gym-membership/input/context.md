# Gym Membership

How consumers choose, use, and feel about gym and fitness-facility memberships.

## About this survey

This survey measures whether respondents have a gym membership, the type of facility, how long they have had it, what they pay, and how often they visit. It captures which facilities and services they use, what influenced their choice, and how they signed up. Likert batteries measure attitudes toward the membership's value, usage, convenience, crowding, and motivation, and a matrix rates satisfaction with equipment, cleanliness, classes, staff, crowding, and value, with a second matrix on the importance of cost, location, variety, and flexible terms. It also covers travel distance, reasons for underuse, app use, paying for unused memberships, renewal intent, overall satisfaction, a likelihood-to-recommend scale, and a top-3 ranking of improvements. Question types include single-choice, multi-select, several Likert scales, matrix batteries split per item, numeric free-text items, and a demographic block covering age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, area type, and self-rated health.
