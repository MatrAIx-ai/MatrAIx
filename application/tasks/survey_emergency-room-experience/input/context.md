# Emergency Room Experience

How people experience hospital emergency room visits, from wait times to communication and cost.

## About this survey

This survey captures respondents' recent emergency room visits at a high level, including how they arrived, why they chose the ER, and how long the visit took. It measures wait times, communication, respect, facility comfort, and billing clarity, along with cost worry and reliance on the ER. Attitudes are captured through satisfaction, agreement, frequency, and likelihood scales, including a satisfaction matrix battery and a 0-to-10 recommendation score. Numeric and open-text items capture visit counts, visit duration, and improvement ideas, followed by a full demographic block including insurance status.
