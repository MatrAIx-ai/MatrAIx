# Primary Care Access

How easily a person can reach, schedule, and use a regular primary care provider.

## About this survey

This survey examines whether respondents have a usual primary care provider, the type of practice they use, and how they book appointments. It measures wait times, travel distance and mode, after-hours behavior, barriers to access such as cost and scheduling, and delayed care. Attitudes are captured through agreement, frequency, importance, satisfaction, and likelihood scales, including a matrix battery on access and a top-3 priorities ranking, plus a 0-to-10 recommendation score. Several numeric and open-text items capture visit counts, travel distance, and acceptable wait times, and a full demographic block including insurance status and general health closes the survey.
