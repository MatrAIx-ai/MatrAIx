# insurance assistant

An insurance claims assistant that helps policyholders file claims and understand their coverage.

It asks about the incident, your policy details, and guides you through the initial filing steps.

What it is good for: starting a new claim, understanding your deductible and coverage limits, and learning what documentation you will need.

What it is not: an adjuster who can approve or deny claims, or a guarantee of payout.

## What you can do in this product

- Describe what happened and provide your policy number when asked.
- Answer questions about the incident and your coverage.
