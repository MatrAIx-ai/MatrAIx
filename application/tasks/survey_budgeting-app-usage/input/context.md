# Budgeting App Usage

How people use budgeting apps and digital tools to manage their spending.

## About this survey

This survey covers whether and how long people use budgeting tools, which apps they rely on (YNAB, Monarch Money, Rocket Money, EveryDollar, Copilot, PocketGuard, Goodbudget, spreadsheets, and bank features), whether they pay for premium versions, how often they check in, and whether accounts are linked for automatic tracking. It measures the features used, budgeting methods (zero-based, 50/30/20, envelopes), the spending categories tracked most closely, and how people discovered their app. Likert and satisfaction batteries capture improved habits, ease of use, trust, and satisfaction with setup, categorization accuracy, syncing, value, and design, plus ranked reasons for using an app and common frustrations. Closing items probe app abandonment, changes in savings, confidence, satisfaction, and intent. A full demographic block records age, gender, race and ethnicity, income, education, employment, marital status, household size, smartphone type, region, and urbanicity.
