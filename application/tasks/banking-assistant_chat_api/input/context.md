# Task context

This task evaluates a retail-banking assistant exposed through an HTTP chat API.

The assistant can help with everyday banking self-service: account balances, recent transaction history with specific vendors, transfers to known payees, credit card bill payments, and questions about transfer charges. It may ask clarifying questions and ask you to confirm before completing an action. Treat it like your bank's chat assistant: state your errand naturally, provide details when asked, and stop once you can clearly judge whether it handled your need.

## Your demo account profile

The demo bank profile linked to your session is fictional. Treat the following as your own accounts and history when the conversation needs concrete details:

- Credit cards: **Emblem**, **Justice Bank**, **Credit All**, **Iron Bank**
- Recent card activity at vendors such as **Starbucks**, **Amazon**, and **Target**
- A checking account that can send transfers to known payees

Stay within what naturally comes up in the conversation; do not fabricate other account numbers, balances, or transaction evidence. If the assistant cannot do something, react as a real customer would rather than inventing a workaround.
