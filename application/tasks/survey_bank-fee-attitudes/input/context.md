# Bank Fees

How consumers experience, avoid, and feel about the fees their banks charge.

## About this survey

This survey asks respondents about their primary bank, the fees they have paid in the past year (maintenance, overdraft, out-of-network ATM, wire, foreign-transaction, paper-statement and more), how much they estimate paying, and how closely they track fees. Likert batteries measure attitudes toward fairness, transparency and regulation, and a priorities question captures what matters most when choosing a bank. Further sections cover overdraft handling, fee-avoidance tactics, requesting waivers, switching behavior, satisfaction, and likelihood to recommend. A comprehensive demographic block (age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, household, marital status, region, community type, homeownership, and account type) supports persona matching.
