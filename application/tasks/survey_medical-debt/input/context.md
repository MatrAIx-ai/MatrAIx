# Medical Debt

Consumers' experiences with medical bills and the burden of medical debt.

## About this survey

This survey covers whether households have struggled with medical bills, how much medical debt they currently owe, the types of care that generated it, and their insurance situation at the time. It measures how people learn about and respond to unpayable bills, including payment plans, negotiation, charity care, credit cards, medical financing, and collections. Likert and impact batteries capture financial stress, billing transparency, and how medical debt affects rent, loan payments, everyday expenses, saving, and credit scores, along with a ranked set of top cost concerns. Closing items gauge disputes, confidence in handling a large bill, preparedness, satisfaction with affordability, and intent to save more. A full demographic block records age, gender, race and ethnicity, income, education, employment, insurance type, marital status, household size, region, and urbanicity.
