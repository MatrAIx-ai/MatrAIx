We're gathering reactions to **Amazon Baby Registry Welcome Box** — an Amazon baby products listing rated 3.7 stars across ~5,691 customer ratings.

Imagine you bought it and it just arrived. One thing to weigh: the listed price recently changed from **$35.00** to **$43.75**. After reading the brief, tell us how likely you'd be to send it back, why, and what would convince you to keep it.

## How to answer
