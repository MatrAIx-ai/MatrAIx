# Subscription Spending

How consumers use, track, and manage recurring subscription services and the money they spend on them.

## About this survey

This survey measures how many paid subscriptions the respondent has and their total monthly spend, then details the categories and specific services they use (video and music streaming, cloud/software, gaming, meal kits, fitness, shopping memberships, and boxes), payment methods, and how closely they track spending. It captures forgotten and canceled subscriptions and the reasons behind cancellations. Likert batteries measure perceived value, spending awareness, cancellation friction, and attitudes toward owning versus subscribing; satisfaction and importance batteries rate value across categories and the criteria used to keep a service; and a "top 3" ranking identifies which subscriptions respondents would protect from cuts. Additional questions cover cost-management tactics, free-trial behavior, spending trends, guilt, and cancellation intent. It closes with a comprehensive demographic block (age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, and urban/rural).
