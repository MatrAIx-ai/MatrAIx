# real-estate assistant

A rental application screening chatbot that helps prospective tenants understand whether they qualify for a property.

It asks about your income, rental history, and other criteria that landlords typically evaluate.

What it is good for: getting a preliminary eligibility check before submitting a full rental application.

What it is not: a guarantee of approval, a credit check, or a substitute for the landlord's formal screening process.

## What you can do in this product

- Tell the assistant which property you are interested in.
- Answer questions about your finances and rental history honestly.
