# Small Business Finance

How small-business owners fund, manage, and feel about their business finances.

## About this survey

This survey asks owners about their business structure, size, age, revenue, and how it was funded, along with whether personal and business finances are kept separate and who handles the books. It captures the accounting tools and financing sources used, cash-flow predictability, and cash runway. Likert batteries measure cash-flow health, financial visibility, financing access, time burden, and decision confidence, while a priorities question ranks current financial goals. Additional sections cover taxes, loan applications, business debt, advice sources, financial health, satisfaction, and optimism, with numeric questions on revenue and debt. A comprehensive demographic block including the owner's role supports persona matching.
