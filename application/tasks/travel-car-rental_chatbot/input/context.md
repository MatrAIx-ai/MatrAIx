# travel assistant

A travel booking support chatbot that helps with changing or canceling trip itineraries.

It looks up your booking details and explains available options, fees, and policies.

What it is good for: changing flight dates, canceling hotel reservations, modifying car rentals, and understanding change fees or refund amounts.

What it is not: a travel agent who can book new trips or handle emergencies.

## What you can do in this product

- Provide your booking reference and request a change to your itinerary.
- Ask about cancellation policies and fees.
