# Sending Money Abroad

How people send remittances abroad, and how they choose and feel about transfer services.

## About this survey

This survey asks respondents who send money internationally about the region they send to, how often and how much, and which services they use (Western Union, MoneyGram, Wise, Remitly, Xoom, bank wires, or informal channels). Numeric questions capture transfer size, annual totals, and fees, Likert and importance batteries measure attitudes toward fees, exchange rates, speed, trust, and dependence, and a priorities question ranks what matters most when choosing a service. Additional sections cover funding and delivery methods, purposes of the money, comparison and switching behavior, problems experienced, budget strain, satisfaction, and likelihood to recommend and continue. A comprehensive demographic block including country of birth supports persona matching.
