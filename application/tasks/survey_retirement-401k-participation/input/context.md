# Retirement 401(k) Participation

How workers participate in, invest through, and feel about employer-sponsored retirement plans.

## About this survey

This survey studies workplace retirement saving from plan access and enrollment through the details of participation — plan type, contribution rate, annual contribution, employer match, total balance, provider, and how contributions are invested. It measures behaviors such as reviewing investments, increasing contributions, taking loans or withdrawals, rollovers, and use of planning resources, then applies agreement and importance Likert batteries to gauge confidence, understanding, worry, and the role of the match. Sub-topics include expected retirement age, retirement confidence, likelihood of increasing contributions, and other expected income sources, followed by plan satisfaction, a 0-to-10 recommendation score, and an optional comment. A comprehensive demographic block records age, gender, race or ethnicity, household income, education, employment, household size, marital status, U.S. region, urban/suburban/rural setting, and employer type.
