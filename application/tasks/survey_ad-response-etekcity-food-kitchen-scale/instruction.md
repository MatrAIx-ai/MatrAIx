# Etekcity Food Kitchen Scale Ad Response Survey

We're gathering reactions to **Etekcity Food Kitchen Scale** — an Amazon kitchen listing rated 4.6 stars across ~175,342 customer ratings.

Imagine this listing just showed up as an ad in your feed. One thing to weigh: the listed price recently changed from **$13.99** to **$17.49**. After reading the brief, tell us whether the ad would stop your scroll, whether you'd believe it, and what you'd do next.

## How to answer

- Read the brief before you start.
- Answer every required question.
- For multiple-choice, use the listed option ids.
- For rating scales, use a whole number in the given range.
- Give the answer alone unless a question also asks for a short reason or confidence.
