# Retail Order-Support Chatbot

Your order **#W-2087** — a pair of running shoes — arrived in the **wrong size**: you
ordered a **US 10** but received a **US 8**. You open the store's support chatbot to fix it.

Your goal: get a **replacement in the correct size (US 10)** — not a refund, not store
credit, not a different shoe.

- Explain the problem in your own words, and give your order number (**#W-2087**) when asked.
- Ask for the correct-size replacement and confirm how and when it will arrive.
- If the assistant offers a refund instead, or can't resolve it, ask for the replacement
  specifically or ask to be handed to a human agent.

Chat as yourself — the assigned persona — the way you would with a real store. Keep going
until the exchange is arranged or clearly refused.
