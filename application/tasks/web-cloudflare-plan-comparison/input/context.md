# Task context

You are responsible for recommending a Cloudflare **Network & CDN** plan for
your organization's public-facing website. Use the organization type or size
in your background when deciding which plan fits best.

Browse the public pricing page at https://www.cloudflare.com/plans/. Compare
the Free, Pro, Business, and Contract plans. Let your organization's needs,
your budget priorities, and other relevant circumstances shape the
recommendation.

Use only plan information visible on the live page. Do not invent quantitative
traffic, compliance, security, support, or uptime requirements that are not
stated in your background or on the page. Your organization may be identified
by either its size or its sector, such as academia or the public sector. Do not
treat sectors as size categories.
