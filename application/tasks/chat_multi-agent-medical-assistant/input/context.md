# Medical-information assistant

A health-information chatbot that people use to describe symptoms or concerns and get clear, cautious guidance in ordinary language.

It may ask follow-up questions before offering suggestions. It is meant for informational conversations — not emergency care, diagnosis theater, or replacing a clinician.

What to expect: careful wording, clarifying questions, and advice that stays appropriately limited when the situation is uncertain.

## What you can do in this product

- Chat in plain language about symptoms, history, or concerns.
- When a skin photo or other medical image would help, you can upload an image along with a short note (the product accepts common photo formats).
- If the assistant asks you to confirm or reject something it produced (for example after image analysis), you can say yes or no and optionally leave a short comment.

Use image upload or validation only when it fits what a real patient would do in the moment — not every turn.
