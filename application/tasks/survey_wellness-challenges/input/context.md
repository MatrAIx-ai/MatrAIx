# Wellness Challenges

Participation in and attitudes toward wellness challenges such as step contests and fitness streaks.

## About this survey

This survey covers whether respondents have joined wellness challenges, their current involvement, the types (steps, fitness streaks, hydration, nutrition, sleep, mindfulness), sources (employer, app, insurer, gym, friends, social media), individual versus team format, motivations, and reward structures, plus the number completed and reward value in the past year, how progress was tracked, apps used (Fitbit, Apple Fitness, Virgin Pulse/Personify Health, Strava, employer platforms), and typical length. It runs Likert batteries on agreement (motivating, fun, habit-building, connection, rewards, data comfort, pressure) and on what makes a challenge appealing, a top-3 motivator ranking, lasting habit change, confidence, frustrations, privacy concern, satisfaction, a 0–10 recommendation score, and rejoin and employer-challenge intent, plus an open comment. Question types include single-choice, multi-select, Likert scales, a matrix battery split per item, a top-3 ranking, and numeric free-text. Demographics captured: age, gender, race/ethnicity, household income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, urban/rural, employer challenge programs, fitness-tracker ownership, and activity level.
