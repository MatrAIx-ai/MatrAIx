# Calendar App Usage

How people use digital calendar apps to manage their time — which apps they choose, what they track, which features they rely on, and how well calendars keep them organized.

## About this survey

This survey covers use of digital calendar apps such as Google Calendar, Outlook Calendar, Apple Calendar, and Fantastical. It measures how long and how often people use a calendar, which apps and access methods they use, whether they keep separate calendars, what they track, event volume, features used, scheduling links, and AI scheduling features. Attitude batteries capture agreement about organization, dependence, and overload, plus a usefulness rating of individual features and a top-three reasons ranking. Later sections cover frustrations, missed events, satisfaction, likelihood to recommend, and switching intent. Demographics captured include age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, mobile platform, household size, region, and area type.
