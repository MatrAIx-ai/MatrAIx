# Hydration Habits

Everyday drinking and hydration habits among adults.

## About this survey

This survey measures how much water people drink, how hydrated they feel, which beverages they rely on, and their main fluid sources. It captures where they drink, water-bottle use and type, morning-water and thirst patterns, whether they set goals, and how they keep track. Attitudes and impacts are measured through agreement, frequency, and importance batteries covering the value of hydration, self-assessed intake, noticing effects, forgetfulness, and the roles of taste, convenience, temperature, and health benefits, plus effects on energy, focus, and mood. It also covers barriers and helpers, exercise and hot-weather effects, flavor additions, and how people judge adequacy, closing with satisfaction, intent, and a top-three helpers ranking. A comprehensive demographic block captures age band, gender, race or ethnicity, household income, education, employment status, household size, marital status, U.S. region, urban/suburban/rural setting, daily activity level, and self-rated health.
