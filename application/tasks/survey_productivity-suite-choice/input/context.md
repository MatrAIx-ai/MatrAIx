# Office & Productivity Suite Choice

Which office and productivity suites people use, how they use their apps, and what they value in collaboration, cost, and features.

## About this survey

This survey captures a person's primary and recently used productivity suites (Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, Apple iWork, LibreOffice, WPS, and others), which component apps they use, and the context and access mode for that use. It measures how they came to the suite, payment source, file formats, real-time collaboration behavior, compatibility issues, and AI assistant use, then applies Likert agreement and importance batteries plus a top-3 application ranking to gauge attitudes toward features, collaboration, value, lock-in, and cost. Later items address switching history, cloud-document privacy concern, satisfaction, future intent, and a likelihood-to-recommend score, with free-text on weekly hours and desired improvements. A comprehensive demographic block captures age, gender, race/ethnicity, household income, education, employment, role or field, household size, relationship status, U.S. region, urban/rural setting, and self-rated technical skill.
