# Net Worth Tracking

How people track their net worth and monitor their overall financial picture.

## About this survey

This survey covers whether and how long people have tracked their net worth, how often they update it, and the tools they use, from personal spreadsheets to apps such as Empower, Monarch Money, and YNAB or bank and brokerage dashboards. It measures whether accounts are linked for automatic updates, which assets and debts are counted, current net worth range, number of accounts tracked, and recent change. Likert and usefulness batteries capture whether tracking improves decisions, motivates saving, causes anxiety, and supports goals like debt payoff, investing, retirement, and major purchases, plus a ranked set of reasons for tracking. Closing items probe goal comparison, confidence in accuracy, financial knowledge, satisfaction, and intent. A full demographic block records age, gender, race and ethnicity, income, education, employment, marital status, household size, homeownership, region, and urbanicity.
