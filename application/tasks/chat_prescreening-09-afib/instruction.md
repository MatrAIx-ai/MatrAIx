# Trial pre-screening chat - Smartwatch Screening for Undiagnosed Atrial Fibrillation (WATCH-AF)

Chat with the clinical-trial pre-screening assistant as yourself, about joining the study "Smartwatch Screening for Undiagnosed Atrial Fibrillation (WATCH-AF)".

You are considering signing up and want to know whether you qualify. Bring a plausible health situation for someone like you and stay consistent with it for the whole chat. Let the assistant lead the screening: answer its questions naturally from that situation, and say you are not sure when it asks for something a real person would not have at hand, such as an exact lab value or date. Do not volunteer your whole history at once unless that is how you naturally talk.

Continue until the assistant gives you its final eligibility assessment. Once you have received that final assessment, thank the assistant and end the conversation - do not keep chatting after the verdict. This is a preliminary screening conversation, not medical advice - keep the details ordinary and consistent rather than dramatic, and do not state precise clinical numbers with false confidence.
