# Remote Work Tools & Setup

The tools, software, and home-office setup people use to work remotely or hybrid — what they rely on, how well it works, and how they feel about remote work.

## About this survey

This survey covers the remote and hybrid work toolkit, including collaboration suites, chat, video conferencing, cloud storage, project management, VPNs, and home-office equipment and internet. It measures work arrangement, remote days, workspace, essential tools and equipment, who provided the gear and personal spend, and connectivity. Attitude batteries capture agreement about effectiveness, productivity, collaboration, and work-life boundaries, plus a satisfaction battery across tools, setup, internet, balance, and connection, and a top-three challenges ranking. Later sections cover what people value about remote work, organizational support, preferred arrangement, overall satisfaction, likelihood to recommend, and expected change. Demographics captured include age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, role, industry, household size, region, and area type.
