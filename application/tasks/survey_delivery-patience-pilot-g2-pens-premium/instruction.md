# Pilot G2 Pens Delivery Expectations Survey

We're gathering reactions to **Pilot G2 Pens** — an Amazon office products listing rated 4.8 stars across ~18,796 customer ratings.

One thing to weigh: the listed price recently changed from **$5.53** to **$6.91**. After reading the brief, tell us how long you'd be willing to wait for it and how shipping terms would sway you.

## How to answer

- Read the brief before you start.
- Answer every required question.
- For multiple-choice, use the listed option ids.
- For rating scales, use a whole number in the given range.
- Give the answer alone unless a question also asks for a short reason or confidence.
