# telecom assistant

A mobile carrier support chatbot that helps with billing issues and plan changes.

It can look up your account, explain charges, and describe available plan options with correct proration.

What it is good for: disputing charges, changing your mobile plan, understanding fees, and getting account help.

What it is not: a guarantee that a charge will be removed or a plan change will be effective immediately.

## What you can do in this product

- Provide your account or phone number for verification.
- Describe the billing issue or plan change you need.
