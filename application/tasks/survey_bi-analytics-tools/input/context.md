# Business Intelligence & Analytics Tools

Which business intelligence and analytics tools people use to explore data and build reports and dashboards, and how satisfied they are.

## About this survey

This survey classifies whether the respondent builds or consumes analytics, then maps the BI tools they use (Power BI, Tableau, Looker, Looker Studio, Qlik, Metabase, Sisense, Domo, ThoughtSpot, QuickSight, Excel, Google Sheets, and others), their primary tool, and usage frequency. It measures analytics use cases, data sources, data warehouse/storage, data-prep methods, SQL comfort, sharing methods, refresh cadence, dashboard count, and AI/automation features, followed by Likert agreement statements, satisfaction and importance batteries split per item, and organizational data maturity. It closes with a top-3 challenges ranking, a likelihood-to-recommend score, overall satisfaction, tool-switch intent, and an open item. Question types include single-choice, multi-select, Likert scales, matrices split per item, a ranking, and numeric free-text. Demographics captured include age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, role/department, industry, company size, work arrangement, U.S. region, and urban/suburban/rural.
