# Task context

Browse the public recipe catalog at https://www.allrecipes.com/.

You are deciding on the **one recipe** you would most realistically cook this
week. Explore as yourself: use search or the category pages, open a few recipe
pages, and weigh what actually matters to you — taste, dietary needs, total
time, ingredient cost, and how confident you feel cooking it.

Read the recipe title and total time from the live page; do not invent values.
