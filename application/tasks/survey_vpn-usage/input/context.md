# VPN Usage

How people use virtual private networks (VPNs) for online privacy, security on public Wi-Fi, and access to restricted content.

## About this survey

This survey examines VPN adoption and behavior: whether respondents use a consumer, free, work, or self-hosted VPN, which services they rely on (NordVPN, ExpressVPN, Surfshark, Proton VPN, Mullvad, and others), and what they use it for. It measures plan type and spending, devices, usage frequency and weekly hours, then uses Likert batteries on privacy, trust, speed, and value plus an importance matrix on selection criteria. Additional sections cover discovery channels, reasons for abandoning a VPN, general privacy concern, free-VPN attitudes, a top-3 reasons ranking, and outcome measures for satisfaction, likelihood to recommend, and renewal intent. The demographic block captures age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, urbanicity, tech comfort, and work situation.
