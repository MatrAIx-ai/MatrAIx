# Task Manager & To-Do Apps

How people use personal task manager and to-do list apps to organize their work and lives — which apps they choose, how they use them, and whether the apps make them more productive.

## About this survey

This survey covers use of task manager apps such as Todoist, Microsoft To Do, Apple Reminders, TickTick, and Things. It measures how long and how consistently people use them, which apps and entry methods they use, what they track, active task counts, features used, task-management methods, review habits, spending, and AI features. Attitude batteries capture agreement about productivity, stress reduction, and follow-through, plus a frequency battery on completing, carrying over, and abandoning tasks, and a top-three reasons ranking. Later sections cover frustrations, switching history, satisfaction, likelihood to recommend, and switching intent. Demographics captured include age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, mobile platform, household type and size, region, and area type.
