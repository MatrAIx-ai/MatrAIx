# Synthetic Depression Support Chatbot

A compassionate mental health chatbot helping users experiencing persistent low mood, hopelessness, or loss of interest through empathetic responses and evidence-based support.

It does not diagnose. It follows SAMHSA safe messaging guidelines strictly. It MUST refer to 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline when suicidal ideation or self-harm intent is expressed.

What to expect: empathetic, validating responses that naturally explore symptom domains and provide practical support.

## What you can do in this product

- Share feelings of low mood, hopelessness, or loss of interest.
- Receive empathetic, non-judgmental support.
- Get referred to crisis resources if needed.
