# LLM Chatbot Usage

How people use AI chatbots powered by large language models, such as ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude.

## About this survey

This survey measures consumer and professional use of AI chatbots, beginning with a screener on usage, tenure, and frequency. It records the primary chatbot and chatbots used, the devices and access methods, the range of tasks chatbots are used for, personal versus work orientation, daily message volume, and spending on subscriptions. Attitudes are captured with an agreement Likert battery on time savings, trust, search replacement, accuracy, and privacy and a per-item importance matrix over chatbot-selection criteria, plus a top-three ranking of uses and a multi-select of concerns. Additional sections cover answer accuracy, trust, fact-checking, voice and image features, effect on search-engine use, and satisfaction with accuracy and usefulness, followed by overall satisfaction, recommendation likelihood, usage-growth intent, and switching intent. Question types include single-choice, multi-select, Likert scales, an importance matrix, a ranking item, and numeric free-text. The survey ends with a comprehensive demographic block covering age, gender, race and ethnicity, household income, education, employment, household size, marital status, U.S. region, urbanicity, occupation, and technology-adoption profile.
