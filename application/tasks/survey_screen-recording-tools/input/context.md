# Screen Recording & Screen-Capture Tools

Which screen recording and screen-capture tools people use, for what purposes, and how satisfied they are.

## About this survey

This survey screens on how often and why the respondent records or captures their screen, then maps the tools they use (OBS Studio, Loom, Camtasia, Snagit, ScreenFlow, QuickTime, Windows Game Bar, macOS tools, ShareX, Bandicam, CleanShot X, Screencastify, Zoom/Teams, and others), their primary tool, operating system, and capture style. It measures what they capture (webcam, audio, region), recording length and monthly volume, post-recording and sharing workflows, annotation features, payment, and spend, followed by Likert agreement and satisfaction batteries and an importance battery on selection factors. It closes with a top-3 feature ranking, frustrations, switching behavior, a likelihood-to-recommend score, overall satisfaction, switch intent, and an open item. Question types include single-choice, multi-select, Likert scales, a satisfaction matrix split per item, a ranking, and numeric free-text. Demographics captured include age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, primary role, work arrangement, household size, U.S. region, and urban/suburban/rural.
