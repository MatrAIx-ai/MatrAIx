# Synthetic Anxiety Support Chatbot

A compassionate mental health chatbot helping users experiencing anxiety through empathetic responses and evidence-based coping strategies.

It does not diagnose. It MUST refer to 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline when suicidal ideation or self-harm intent is expressed.

What to expect: empathetic, non-judgmental responses with practical coping strategies tailored to the user's stated symptoms and severity.

## What you can do in this product

- Share anxiety symptoms and receive empathetic support.
- Learn evidence-based coping strategies (CBT, grounding, breathing).
- Get referred to crisis resources if needed.
