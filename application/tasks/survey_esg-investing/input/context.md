# ESG Investing

How investors understand, use, and feel about ESG and sustainable investing.

## About this survey

This survey measures familiarity with ESG and sustainable investing, whether respondents hold such investments and in what share of their portfolio, and which values (environmental, social, governance) matter most. Likert, trust, and importance batteries capture beliefs about returns, risk, greenwashing, and the weight given to impact versus performance, and a priorities question ranks what matters most in an ESG investment. Additional sections cover investment types held, barriers, research methods, employer-plan options, the effect of political debate, recent portfolio changes, satisfaction, and likelihood to recommend and increase ESG holdings. A comprehensive demographic block including investing experience supports persona matching.
