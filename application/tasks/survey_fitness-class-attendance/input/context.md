# Fitness Class Attendance

How consumers take part in group fitness classes.

## About this survey

This survey covers whether respondents attend group fitness classes, how many per week, and which types (yoga, Pilates, spin, HIIT, strength, dance, water aerobics, martial arts). It measures where they attend (gym, boutique studio, community center, home streaming, outdoors, workplace), how long they have attended, how they pay (membership, packages, drop-in, multi-studio apps), monthly spend, booking method, reasons for attending, time of day, travel distance, and instructor influence. It runs Likert batteries on attitudes (motivation, social value, worth the cost, instructor importance, schedule fit, feeling welcome) and on the importance of class-selection factors, plus a top-3 ranking of those factors, things that would boost attendance, reasons for skipping or stopping, attendance consistency, use of tracking apps or wearables, goal fit, confidence, satisfaction, intent to continue, a 0–10 recommendation score, and an open comment. Question types include single-choice, multi-select, Likert scales, a matrix battery split per item, a top-3 ranking, and numeric free-text. Demographics captured: age, gender, race/ethnicity, household income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, urban/rural, fitness level, and gym or studio membership.
