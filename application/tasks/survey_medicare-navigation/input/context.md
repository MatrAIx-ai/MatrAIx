# Medicare Navigation

How people understand, compare, and make decisions about Medicare coverage.

## About this survey

This survey examines how respondents navigate Medicare, including their coverage type, prescription coverage, and the resources they use for help. It measures which parts of Medicare are confusing, how easy plans are to compare, and understanding of enrollment deadlines. Attitudes are captured through agreement, frequency, importance, satisfaction, and likelihood scales, including an agreement matrix battery, a top-3 decision-factor ranking, and a 0-to-10 recommendation score. Numeric and open-text items capture plans compared and points of confusion, followed by a full demographic block.
