# Health Coaching

Consumer experience with and attitudes toward health and wellness coaching services.

## About this survey

This survey covers whether respondents have worked with a health or wellness coach, their current status, the goals they sought help with (weight, nutrition, fitness, sleep, stress, routines, conditions), how coaching was accessed (employer, insurer, app, gym, independent), the meeting format and frequency, monthly cost, duration, programs used (Noom, WW, Omada, employer apps), how they were matched, and sessions in the past month. It runs Likert batteries on agreement (understanding, accountability, personalization, motivation, progress, scheduling, value) and on the importance of factors when choosing a coach, a top-3 most-valued-aspect ranking, perceived progress, confidence in maintaining changes, drawbacks, responsiveness, satisfaction, a 0–10 recommendation score, and continuation and free-benefit intent, plus an open comment. Question types include single-choice, multi-select, Likert scales, a matrix battery split per item, a top-3 ranking, and numeric free-text. Demographics captured: age, gender, race/ethnicity, household income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, urban/rural, insurance status, employer wellness benefits, and self-rated health.
