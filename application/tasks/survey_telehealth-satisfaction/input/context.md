# Telehealth Satisfaction

How satisfied people are with the virtual (phone or video) medical visits they have experienced.

## About this survey

This survey focuses on the quality and satisfaction of respondents' actual telehealth experiences, including visit type, reason, wait time, and technical issues. It captures what went well and what was frustrating, and rates specific aspects of care such as scheduling, audio and video quality, and provider communication. Attitudes are measured with agreement, frequency, satisfaction, and likelihood scales, including a satisfaction matrix battery and a 0-to-10 recommendation score. Numeric and open-text items capture visit counts and improvement ideas, and a full demographic block including insurance status closes the survey.
