# Insurance Marketplace Plans

How people choose, enroll in, and evaluate health plans from the government insurance marketplace.

## About this survey

This survey focuses on respondents' experience with HealthCare.gov and state marketplaces, including metal tiers, subsidies, and enrollment help. It measures which parts of enrollment are challenging, which plan features people compare, and how easy it is to confirm coverage. Attitudes are captured through agreement, frequency, importance, satisfaction, and likelihood scales, including an agreement matrix battery, a top-3 priorities ranking, and a 0-to-10 recommendation score. Numeric and open-text items capture plans compared and improvement ideas, followed by a full demographic block.
