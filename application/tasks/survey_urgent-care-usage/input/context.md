# Urgent Care Usage

When and how people use urgent care and walk-in clinics for non-emergency needs.

## About this survey

This survey measures how often respondents use urgent care, the types of clinics they choose, and the reasons they pick urgent care over primary care or the emergency room. It covers travel, wait times, payment, and how people decide where to go. Attitudes are captured through agreement, frequency, importance, satisfaction, and likelihood scales, including a satisfaction matrix battery, a top-3 selection-factor ranking, and a 0-to-10 recommendation score. Numeric and open-text items capture visit counts, wait times, and improvement ideas, followed by a full demographic block including insurance status.
