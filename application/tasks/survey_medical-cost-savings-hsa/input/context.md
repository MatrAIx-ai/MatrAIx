# Health Savings Account (HSA) Usage

How consumers use Health Savings Accounts and otherwise save for medical costs.

## About this survey

This survey covers whether respondents have a high-deductible health plan and an HSA, where the account is held (Fidelity, HealthEquity, Optum Bank, Lively, and others), how long they have had it, and how it was opened. It measures contribution amounts, employer contributions, whether the balance is spent, saved, or invested, current balances, and the specific expenses HSA funds cover. Likert and importance batteries capture understanding of the tax advantages, peace of mind, worry about large bills, and whether the account is treated as a long-term retirement vehicle, along with a ranked set of reasons for holding an HSA. Additional items probe other medical-savings strategies, delayed care, and trouble paying bills, then close with knowledge, confidence, satisfaction, and intent. A full demographic block records age, gender, race and ethnicity, income, education, employment, marital status, household size, insurance type, region, and urbanicity.
