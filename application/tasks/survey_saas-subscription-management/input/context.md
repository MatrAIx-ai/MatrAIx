# SaaS & App Subscription Management

How people sign up for, track, pay for, review, and cancel their software and app subscriptions.

## About this survey

This survey measures the number and categories of software and app subscriptions people pay for, how much they spend, and how they keep track of billing across services. It explores behaviors around trials, cancellation, price increases, auto-renewals, bundles, and shared plans, and attitudes such as subscription fatigue and ownership-versus-rental preferences. It uses agreement-scale and importance-scale Likert batteries, a per-item importance matrix on subscription decision factors, a "top 3 priorities" ranking, and numeric free-text items on annual spending and cancellations. It closes with satisfaction, future intent, a 0-10 control scale, and a full demographics block including age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, household, region, locale, and budgeting style.
