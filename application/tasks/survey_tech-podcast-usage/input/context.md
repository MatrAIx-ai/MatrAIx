# Tech Podcast Usage

How people listen to technology, software, and developer podcasts — the apps, genres, habits, and attitudes behind their listening.

## About this survey

This survey profiles tech podcast listening from frequency and preferred apps through genres, discovery methods, listening contexts, playback speed, and episode-length preferences. It measures how many shows respondents follow, how they handle ads and paid tiers, whether they watch video versions, and how their listening is trending. Likert agreement and podcast-selection importance batteries capture what people value in a show and how connected they feel to hosts, while other items track note-taking, ad behavior, and completion. Question types include single-choice, multi-select, several Likert scales, split matrix batteries, a top-3 genre ranking, numeric and open free-text, plus satisfaction, recommendation, and future-intent items, followed by a full demographic block covering age, gender, race/ethnicity, education, employment, tech relationship, income, household, relationship status, region, and area type.
