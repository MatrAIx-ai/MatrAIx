# Personal Budgeting Habits

How individuals and households plan, track, and stick to a personal or household budget.

## About this survey

This survey explores whether respondents keep a budget, how long they have done so, and which methods and tools they rely on — from apps like YNAB, EveryDollar, and Rocket Money to spreadsheets and cash envelopes. It measures tracking and review frequency, savings rate, budget adherence, and how people handle unexpected expenses, alongside Likert batteries on budgeting attitudes and the importance of goals such as emergency funds, debt payoff, and retirement. Multi-select and top-3 priority questions capture categories budgeted, control tactics, and advice sources, while numeric free-text items record grocery, dining, and take-home income amounts. A comprehensive demographic block covers age, gender, race or ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, community type, and homeownership.
